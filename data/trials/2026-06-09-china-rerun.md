## Rejected Under New Rule

- Medical device approval without use or patient outcome.
- Medical AI entering special review channel.
- Environmental release-only activity with no survival/monitoring data.
- Policy action launch without local project outcome.
- Unopenable links.

## Rerun Conclusion

The stricter Chinese rule works. It filters out most announcement-style news and
surfaces fewer but better candidates:

- 1 strong publishable candidate: “熊猫侠” rare-blood volunteer network.
- 1 community-service candidate that needs a second source: cold-water/shatan elderly services.
- 1 NGO profile/follow-up candidate with strong numbers but self-report risk.

For Chinese daily content, the strongest themes are:

- long-running volunteer networks with cumulative outcomes;
- community services with regular operation and service counts;
- NGO project reports with follow-up data;
- medical/public-aid items only after actual beneficiary results appear.
