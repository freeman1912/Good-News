## Rule Adjustment Made Today

Chinese webpage candidates can enter the review pool, but automatic AI drafts now
hold:

- official-risk Chinese sources;
- Chinese primary organizational/project sources;
- lectures, trainings, meetings, contests, ceremonies, and partnership PR;
- early-stage medical/science items signaled by "有望", "潜力", "研究显示",
  "研制", "首片", "核心材料", or similar terms;
- Chinese items below a higher evidence/public-value/specificity threshold.

This keeps the pipeline useful without turning the homepage into a Chinese
positive-energy or institutional bulletin feed.
